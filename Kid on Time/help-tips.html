Can I switch to other apps while this app is running? What about locking the phone?

Yes. Bedtime Balloons keeps track of the time even when running in the background or when the phone is locked.

Timing Tips

Work with your child to create a realistic schedule that he or she can follow. Keep tweaking the time as needed.

A minimum time of one minute can prevent a child from immediately skipping through a step.

Why does this app ask to send me notifications?

When running in the background, this app will send you a notification if a step's time is running out.

If you want notifications but are not getting them, open the Settings app and find this app under Notification Center. Then enable notifications for this app.

How can I play music from another app while running through a routine?

Just start music from the other app and then switch back to Bedtime Balloons. The music will keep playing as long as the other app allows it.

Is there a way to pause a routine?

Yes, tap the screen twice with two fingers to pause the routine. This is a hidden feature, and may not want to share it with the kids.

Can I use this app to help with chores or homework?

Yes. Just select the "Use My Own Pictures" option when creating a routine. Then add pictures of chores, homework, or whatever you want.

Bedtime Balloons helps your child get ready for bed by following a series of steps.

When your child completes a step, he or she is rewarded with balloons for later.

Each step can have a time limit.

Watch out for the flying bug. When the time runs out, he starts popping balloons!

When all the tasks are complete, your child is given a score and some balloons of their own to pop, depending on their score.

You can create your own task with your pictures.

Or you can skip the monkey and focus entirely on your photos.
